About us

Who we are

Discover Berlin was founded in 2018 with the aim of giving English-speaking visitors an insight into the city of Berlin and enthusing them for our cultural offer.
Our team has grown over the years. When it was founded, there were three of us. Our company now has 22 employees.
Use the contact form if you want to join our team. We will get back in touch with you.


We are located in the heart of Berlin. Have a look and visit us!
